Cross Border Simulation The full process

The game simulates a trading situation where the players have to optimally and profitably dispatch assets in multiple markets, trade power, execute cross-border arbitrage and minimise their CO2 production.

The Participants will gain an appreciation of:

  • Wholesale market for electricity trading.

  • Types of generation plants and merit order.

  • Buying and selling on Power exchanges.

  • Bids & Offers and market depth.

  • The reason for electricity trading and hedging.

  • Physical Balancing & Optimisation of Assets.

  • Arbitrage opportunities.

  • The challenge of renewables.

  • Physical position management

  • New NordPool simulation.

Game structure

  • Power plant scheduling.

  • Operating the power plants respective to the merit order.

  • Asset back transactions and balancing.

  • Carbon balancing.

  • Buying capacity with other countries.

  • Flowing power cross border.

  • Arbitrage opportunities between countries.

  • Game Result and report.
