Long Term Simulation The full process

​The game simulates a trading situation where the players are in charge of a 420 MW Coal fired PowerStation with the objective to make the highest profit while making sure the correct hedging strategies are used. The players will have to manage generation, counterparty and market price risk and ensure the power station has the require coal stocks and carbon balance.

The Participants will gain an appreciation of:

  • Wholesale market for electricity trading.

  • Core electricity trading products.

  • The reason for electricity trading and hedging.

  • The complexities of hedging a coal-fired power station in the long-term markets.

  • The impact of credit constraints on hedging activities.

  • The impact of carbon and renewables.

  • Different types of spreads.

Game structure

  • Trading a one-year period.

  • Split into calendar quarters.

  • Settlement report at the end of each quarter.

  • Three legs: power, coal and carbon (trading the Clean Dark Spread)

  • Find profitable opportunity and sell the power.

  • Commit the generation.

  • Buy the coal to co​ver the generation.

  • Buy the carbon if required.

  • Watch coal stock limits.

  • Manage counterparty credit.
