Prop Trading Simulation The full process

The game simulates a prop traders experience, trading energy derivatives against the click on an electronic trading platform. The players do not have nay physical assets so they will need to speculate at rising/ falling prices to maximize revenues.

The Participants will gain an appreciation of:

  • Analysis the market leading to buying or selling according to profitable trading strategies.

  • Reacting to market changes.

  • Buying and selling futures and options..

  • Risks associated with trading activities.

  • Trends and technical analysis.

Game structure

  • Trading a five month period.

  • Players can trade all months that lay ahead of the current month.

  • 2 minutes for every trading day.

  • Positions should always be balanced at the end of the trading period i.e. sales and purchases must balance to zero.
