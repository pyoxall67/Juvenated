Short Term Simulation The full process

The game simulates a trading situation where the players are in charge of a 400 MW CCGT power plant which follows the two day short-term trading process on the German electricity market​​. The objective of the game is to optimise the power plant and profit

German market Day One

  • Player runs the morning shift on day one

  • Player receives market view information from analysts at 7:00

  • Auction deadline at 10:30am to send day ahead bids to German market

  • Schedule despatched to transmission system operator by 14:30

  • Intraday platform opens at 15:30 for optimis​ation opportunities

German market Day Two

  • Player runs afternoon shift on day two

  • Player receives market view information from analysts at 14:00

  • Player can optimise individual hours in the intraday market​, react to weather changes and take any OTC trades that make sense

Game structure

  • Power plant scheduling

  • React to anlayst forecasts

  • Operating the power plant respective to the marginal costs

  • Placement of offers for the day ahead auction

  • Reaction to changes due to renewable energies availability

  • Day-ahead-market optimisation

  • Optimization of the portfolio through plant operation

  • Intra-Day optimisation

  • Plant optimisation as a reaction to unplanned events

  • Balancing to avoid penalties
